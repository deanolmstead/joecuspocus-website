I Bring the Party to You

JoecusPocus has been creating joy at birthday parties, wedding receptions and major events for five years now. The company is basically me, Joe, who can arrive with my balloon animal friends for your kids.

But not just that! I can and will bring a photo booth for your memories, a foam party for the summertime, and oodles of bubbles – big and small – to decorate your atmosphere. No extra charge.

There is also the 360 Photo Booth that is either an added charge or an event unto itself. People love it!

I work closely with Fresno Fancy Faces so if you are looking for face painting as well, just let me know! Also, please book well in advance because I and other balloon people get very busy but I will be happy to help you find someone if you get stuck. JoecusPocus is available for booking at (559) 289-7954
